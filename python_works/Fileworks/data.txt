Discipline is one of the most important qualities a person can develop in life. 
It means having self-control, following rules, 
and doing the right thing even when it is difficult. 
A disciplined person manages time wisely, 
stays focused on goals, and avoids distractions.
Discipline helps in studies, work, and personal life by building habits of consistency and responsibility. It also teaches respect for others and creates a sense of order in society. 
Without discipline, success becomes harder to achieve, while with discipline, even the toughest challenges can be overcome
